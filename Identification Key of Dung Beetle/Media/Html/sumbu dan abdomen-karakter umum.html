ABDOMEN STRUCTURES

1. Axis (Body Direction)
	Anterior		: Direction toward the head (front).
	Posterior		: Direction toward the tail/rear (back).
	Dorsal		: The back side (top).
	Ventral		: The belly side (bottom).
	Lateral		: The side part (right or left).
	Apical		: The tip part furthest from the base of the body.
	Basal		: The base part closest to the body.

2. Abdomen (The belly part or the last body part of an insect)
	Pygidium	: The rear end of an insect (the last segment of the abdomen).
	Setae		: Stiff hair or bristle on the insect's body.
