HEAD STRUCTURES

1. Axis (Body Direction)
	Anterior				: Direction toward the head (front).
	Posterior				: Direction toward the tail/rear (back).
	Dorsal				: The back side (top).
	Ventral				: The belly side (bottom).
	Lateral				: The side part (right or left).
	Apical				: The tip part furthest from the base of the body.
	Basal				: The base part closest to the body.

2. Head (The first body part of an insect)
	Clypeus				: Hard plate on the front of the insect's face, right above the lip.
	Gena				: The cheek area of an insect, located below or in front of the eyes.
	Frontal carina (Ridge)	: A prominent line running across the forehead/between the two eyes.
	Horn				: A hard projection structure extending outward (Identifier for Male or Female).
